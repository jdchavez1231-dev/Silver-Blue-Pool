Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Silver Blue Pool Care ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a quote request or contact us, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Zip code and pool details

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your quote or service request
  • Schedule and manage your pool service
  • Send service reminders or follow-up communications

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Retention

We retain your contact information for as long as you are an active customer or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion at any time.

4. Your Rights (CCPA / Nevada Privacy)

Nevada residents and California consumers have the right to:

  • Know what personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of the sale of personal data (we do not sell data)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at service@silverbluepool.com with the subject line "Privacy Request."

5. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics at this time.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at:
service@silverbluepool.com
